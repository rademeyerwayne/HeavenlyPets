Our story

A little pet shop with a big heart

Heavenly Pets PETSHOP is a family-run pet shop in Meyerton, here for the dogs, cats and small companions that make our homes complete. We keep our shelves stocked with the food and essentials you rely on, and we're always happy to share honest, no-pressure advice.

Around the shop we've grown a warm little community on Facebook, where we post new stock, pets looking for homes, seasonal specials and plenty of adorable photos. Whether you're a first-time pet parent or an old hand, you're welcome here.
